About Us

MTSE (Movies and TV Shows Explorer), is a web platform that integrates data from streaming services Netflix and Disney, correlated with additional information available in The Movie Database (TMDb). Its purpose is to provide users with an interactive and informative experience regarding the content available on these platforms, enriched with relevant statistics and visualizations.
